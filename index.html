Menglei Chen

Ph.D. Student in Computer System and Architecture
Huazhong University of Science and Technology


Biography: Menglei Chen is a Ph.D student in Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) advised by Prof. Yu Hua. He obtained the B.E. degree in Computer Science from HUST in 2022. His current research interests include storage systems, deep learning systems, and persistent memory.
